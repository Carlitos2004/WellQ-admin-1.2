## API Endpoints

### Business Intelligence
- `GET /api/business-intelligence` - Fetches business intelligence data.

### Clinic Management
- `POST /api/clinic` - Creates a new clinic.
- `GET /api/clinic/{id}` - Retrieves clinic details.
- `PUT /api/clinic/{id}` - Updates clinic information.

### Plans and Pricing
- `GET /api/plans` - Fetches available plans and pricing options.
- `POST /api/plans` - Creates a new pricing plan.

### Platform Operations
- `GET /api/platform-ops` - Fetches operational status and metrics.

### Infrastructure Status
- `GET /api/infrastructure/status` - Retrieves current infrastructure status.
